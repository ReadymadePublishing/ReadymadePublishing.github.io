Editorial

This week has seen Reggio Emilia win the Italian championship for the 5th time in it's history, whilst Christophe Dheur won the Coupe de Belgique and a lot of club activity in Spain.

Headlines

Reggio Emilia win Italian Club League

Reggio Emilia topped the Italian Serie A, finishing ahead of Eagles Napoli and Coppa Italia winners Black & Blue Pisa in San Bendetto del Tronto. Ascoli lost a dramatic playoff against Stella Artois Milan to lose their place in the division, to join fellow relegated clubs CCT Roma and Fighers Napoli in Serie B next season. Christophe Dheur takes Coupe de Belgique title

SC Charleroi’s Christophe Dheur has won the Belgian Individual Cup, beating Ludovic Ponthieux (Verviers-Herve) in the final. The veteran category title was taken by Temploux’s Benoît Massart, with SC Eugies’ Maxime Rairoux lifting the U15 trophy. Google translate version
