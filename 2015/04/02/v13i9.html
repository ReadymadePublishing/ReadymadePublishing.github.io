Editorial

It's been a good week for Portuguese player Sergio Loureiro, beating the rarely-spotted - but highly talented - Spaniard Raul Benita in the final. Meanwhile, Yorkshire Phoenix took the UK & Ireland Club Championship title, with Scotsman Malcolm Lees winning the individual title.

Headlines

Sergio Loureiro wins Lisbon GP

Details are a bit sparse on the event, but local player Sergio Loureiro defeated Spaniard Raul Benita in the final of the open category. Google Translate translation

Victory for Yorkshire Phoenix and Malcolm Lees at UK & Ireland Championship

Yorkshire Phoenix beat Manchester TFC 2-0 in the final of the UK & Ireland Club Championship on Saturday to lift the team title, whilst Scotsman Malcolm Lees topped the individual championship on Sunday.
