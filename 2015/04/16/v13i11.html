Headlines

Kostas Sesses wins all Greek final in Glasgow

Kostas Sesses defeated fellow Greek Dimitris Kosmopoulous 1-0 in the final of the Glasgow GP to take home the open category title, whilst Perugia’s Stefano de Francesco eased himself to victory in the veteran tournament. The plate competition was won by his Perugia teammate, Scotsman Mike Burns, making his return to the Scottish game after a controversial ban. Athinaikos won the team event, defeating London & Essex United on goal difference after a 2-2 draw.
