Editorial

Mongi Rouis's drop to 2nd place in the Singapore Rankings doesn't seem to have hit his ability on the pitch, the Frenchman beating off a strong field at the IO Puylaurens. Meanwhile, there's been double success for Ciudad de Murcia in Spain, and a surprise victory for German player Marcus Tilgner.

Headlines

Perfect Weekend for Mongi Rouis

Mongi Rouis won the IO Puylaurens in France last weekend, topping his group ahead of favourite Axel Donval, before defeating his brother Fayçal, Jean- Marie Amberny and Belgian Olivier Pére to lift the title. FTC Issy won the team event on Sunday, beating AS Hennuyer in the final.

Miguel Angel López wins Copa de España

Ciudad de Murcia’s Miguel Angel López has won the 6th edition of the Spanish Cup in Talavera de la Reina, beating David Gonzalez in the final, with Murcia also taking the club title, beating FM Sharks on goal difference after a 1-1 draw. Google translate version

Shock victory as Marcus Tilgner claims German title

Berlin-based player Marcus Tilgner has claimed the title of Deutscher Meister for the 2014/15 season, denying Bjorn Kegenbein his 7th successive national title. Elsewhere, Arnold Mair picked up the veteran title, whilst Kai Hagenkötter strolled to victory in the Under 19 category. Google translate version
