Belgian GP titles for Haas and Rochefort

Austrian star Christian Haas won last weekend’s Belgian Grand Prix in Rochefort, with Rochefort TS winning the event hosted by their local rivals, Rochefort JSC.

King Carlos takes Carpi Open crown

Carlos Flores beat Luca Zambelllo, the rising star of Italian Subbuteo, by an incredible 7-5 scoreline to take the Carpi Open title, the high-scoring pair having averaged scoring 4 goals each per game during the knockout stages. Fiamme Azzure won the team event. Google translate
