Editorial

It's been a week of domination in Subbuteo tournaments, with FISTF events in Argentina and England seeing players pick up trophies in multiple events, whilst both the team and open categories in the French National Championships saw their victors take the honours with 100% records.

Headlines

Full honours for Claudio Murcilli in Rosario

Claudio Murcilli struck twice to lift both the Open and Veteran category titles in the International Open of Argentina in Rosario, beating Fabrizio Bertolini on both occasions.

Remi Soret wins Championnats de France

Remi Soret walked to victory in the French national championships last weekend, topping the first division ahead of Jean-Marie Amberny and Axel Donval having won every game. Meanwhile, FTC Issy-les-Moulineaux won the team championship, having also won every match, with FTC Puylaurens in second position. Bretagne won the regional category, with Pays de Loire in second place and France Mixte in third.

Double vision for Martinelli at English Satellite weekender

Luca Martinelli beat Cypriot Paul Andreas 3-1 on Saturday, then defeated Colin Tarry 4-1 on Sunday, to take both open category titles in the English Satellite weekender in Enderby, near Leicester, last weekend.
