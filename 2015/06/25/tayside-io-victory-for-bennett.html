International Edition

Editorial

Internationally, it's been a bit of a quiet week, with only a Steve Bennett victory in Dundee being of note. However, the week hasn't been without interest outside of that, with former US Champion Rick Wilcox making a welcome return to the game, whilst former World Champion Vasco Guimarães has announced that he will take part in the 2015 World Cup, blaming the regularity of them for his absence in the event over the past few seasons.

Headlines

Home win for Bennett in Tayside IO

Local player Steve Bennett defeated England’s Trevor Schott in the final of the Interntational Open of Tayside to add another Scottish title to his expanding collection. Bennett picked up further honours in the following day, with his club - Dundee United TFC - taking home the team trophy.
