Editorial

Perhaps understandably, given the Bologna Major was held last weekend, there's been precious little Subbuteo action this week throughout most of Europe. It has, however, seen great strides forward for three associations which perhaps wouldn't be regularly associated with eachother - Italy, whose has surpassed 1000 members, a record breaking World Cup Squad for the USA, and a cosmopolitan entry field for the International Open of Singapore..

Headlines

Results from the Bologna Major released

Bologna Tigers, still going strong in their 40th year, have released the results from the Major, along with a full report being published by ItaliaSubbuteo.

Singapore getting set for International Open

Indicative of the surge in popularity for the game in the far east, the governing body of the game in Singapore expect 38 players from seven nations - Singapore, Japan, Australia, Italy, France, Belgium, and Malaysia - to compete in it’s International Open this weekend. Alongside the usual category games will be international friendlies in both Open and U15 categories between Singpore and Japan.

USA Announces World Cup Squad

The USA has announced that it will send a record 8 players to the FISTF World Cup this season, including teams in both the open and veteran categories. Captained by Zach Walker, the squad also includes former England Champion Paul O’Donovan Rossa.
