Thursday, 4 June 2015

International Edition

Volume 13, Issue 18

Editorial

Juan Noguera added the Bologna Major title to his existing World Champion one, with a fantastic victory against Daniel Bertelli. Meanwhile, Gibraltan player John Field stormed to victory in his home GP, taking both the Open and Veteran titles in style.

Headlines

Juan Noguera wins Bologna Major

Spaniard Juan Nogera won the Bologna Major last weekend, beating Daniel Bertelli in the final. Over 200 players entered the event in San Benedetto del Tronto, along with 48 teams in the team category. Full results are, as of yet, unavailable.

Double for John Field in Gibraltar

31 players from five nations attended the GP Gibraltar last weekend, with local players John Field and Patrick Dean contesting the final, Field coming out 3-1 winner after seeing of Spaniards Antonio Montano and Juan Carlos Granados in the earlier rounds. He followed it up by claiming a 3-0 victory over Dean in the Veteran category, on that occasion having dispatched Nuno Noronha and England’s Colin Tarry. David Gonzalez won the U19 category, whilst Portuguese youngster Gil Afonso won the U15 category, whilst FM Tiburones won the team title.
