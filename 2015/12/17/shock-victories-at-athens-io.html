Editorial

In the week that Subbuteo creator Peter Adolph would've celebrated his 99th birthday, he would surely be delighted to see over 70 players attending a tournament in Greece, with surprise winners in both the team and individual categories, whilst a tournament for one of his more obscure creations - Subbuteo Hockey - was announced in the UK, along with a bar opening in Manchester with a Subbuteo table on-site! Not bad for a 68 year old game (or 86 if you count the very-similar game Newfooty)!

Headlines

Shock victories at Athens IO

Olympia claimed a surprise 3-1 victory against Athens Falcons in Greek IO to lift the trophy last weekend, in a tournament overshadowed by the death of veteran player Dimitri Sorotou. Sixteen year old John Vlassopoulos won the open category, topping a field of over 70 with a 3-0 victory over Cite Patras in the final.
