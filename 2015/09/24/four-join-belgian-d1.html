Editorial

Spain's domination of the game in the open category continues, with Juan Noguero picking up the FISTF Player of the Year Award, whilst in a strange twist of fate Valentina Vezzali's request for Subbuteo to be brought to schools seems to have been heard in Australia, seeing an introduction in to the Toongabbie Christian School.

Headlines

Four join Belgian Division 1

Michaël Casciano made a triumphant return to the top flight of the Belgian league last weekend, topping the qualification tournament table, with Philippe Roufosse (2nd), Geoffrey Marain (3rd), and Delphine Dieudonné (4th) joining him. The tournament, hosted by AS Hennuyer, saw 46 players compete for places in the Belgian Division 1, to be hosted in Eugies and Templeuve, along with a third venue which is yet to be decided.
