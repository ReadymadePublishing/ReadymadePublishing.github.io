Thursday, 26 February 2015

International Edition

Volume 13, Issue 4

Editorial

This week has been dominated by team tournaments, with Italy, Malta, Spain and Austria all hosting their national club cups. The highest profile was the Coppa Italia, which saw Black & Blue Pisa defeat Fiamme Azzure in the final.

Headlines

Black & Blue Pisa win the Coppa Italia [it] (Google Translate)

The 19th edition of the Coppa Italia has been won by Black & Blue Pisa for the third time in the club’s history, after impressive victories over ACS Perugia (3-0), Eagles Napoli (3-1) and Fiamme Azzurre Roma (1-0) from a huge 33-team field.

Tiburones FM win Spanish Club Championships [es] (Google Translate)

Tiburones FM beat Cap Ciudad de Murcia to lift the team championships in Murcia, whilst David Gonzalez saw off Jose M. Castellano in the Open category final in a field of 18.

TSC Royal 78 & Valletta SC also triumph

Elsewhere in Europe, TSC Royal 78 Kaisermuhlen defeated TFC Mattersburg to lift the Austrian Cup, whilst Valletta SC claimed the Maltese title with a victory against local rivals Valletta Lions.
