Editorial

Both International Opens play last weekend saw the open category winners also take home the team trophies, with Athens Falcons (Greece) and Tiburones FM (Spain) victorious. Outside of those, it's been a quiet week for table football, although this weekend sees the 34-club Coppa Italia take place in San Benedetto del Tronto!

Headlines

Star of David Shines Bright in Portugal

Young Spanish star David Gonzalez lifted the Open and U19 titles at the IO Tires in Portugal at the weekend, whilst his club, Tiburones FM, won the team event. Defeated Open finalist Nuno Noronha consoled himself with the veteran category trophy, whilst Gil Afonso won the Under 15 category.

Falcons soar in IO Athens

Athens Falcons player Spyros Hantzaras beat Athinaikos’ Kostas Seses on penalties after a goalless final to lift the open category trophy in Parnitha on Sunday, after his club beat Scarlet Battalion SC in the team final the previous day. Atlas boss Lazaros Papakonstantinou won the veteran tournament, whilst Dionysia Koulosousa (Pobladores Griegos) topped a group of 5 the ladies category. Yannis Salamouris won the consolation tournament.
