Editorial

The ongoing Eurozone crisis seems to have done nothing to distract Athens Falcons, who lifted the FISTF Major title in their home city. The story, however, has been almost overshadowed by Luca Colangelo's surprise victory at the IO Catania, the youngster seeing off some strong opposition to lift the title.

Headlines

Falcons soar in Athens Major

Athens Falcons stormed to victory amongst a field of a Greek record 22 teams, beating Olympia in the final of the team event to lift the title. Meanwhile, Spryos Hantzaras won the open category, defeating local legend George Koutis 1-0 in the final. Edoardo Bellotto (Veterans), Elodie Bertholet (Female), Nicolo Borgo (U19), and Marcos Falelakis (U12) also took home silverware.

Sensational victory for Luca Colangelo at IO Catania

1-0 down with five minutes to go against Malta’s Hansel Mallia, Italian youngster struck twice to win the IO Catania. Fiamme Azzurre Roma won the team event, beating the home side 3-1 in the final. Google translate version

Victory for Prats in Barcelona

Last weekend saw Vicenç Prats take the historic Barcelona IO title, defeating Ferran Coli 2-1 in the final. However, he failed to make it a double, losing by a single goal to Antonio Montaño in the veterans category.
