Editorial

With the table football season gearing down for the summer, it's been a slow news week on the tournament front. However, David González has bolstered his reputation as a leading light in the Spanish game, no mean feat considering the strong field. One of those, Carlos Flores, has been interviewed in an article published for the World Cup website, whilst a slew of information coming from Italy regarding both transfers and the forthcoming World Championships.

Eagles Napoli lose the fight for Di Vito

Italian starlet Marco Di Vito has made a cross-city move from Eagles Napoli to rivals Fighters Napoli. Having been part of Eagles’ Europa Cup for Clubs-winning side, and schooled by Massimo Bolognino and Massimiliano Nastasi, the move comes as quite a coup for Fighters. Google translate version
