Editorial

Early reports suggest that the Haas brothers have had rather a lot of success in the past fortnight, with Alexander winning the Austrian IO, and Christian taking the Spanish GP, whilst finding time to lift a WASPA open in between.

Headlines

Zambello crowned Champion of Italy

Pierce Monferrato player Luza Zambello won the Italian National Champioship last weekend, beating Massimo Bolognino 4-3 in the final. Meanwhile, Massimilliano Croati took the veteran title, whilst Salvatore Mandanici claimed victory in the youth category. Google translate version.

SC Charleroi top Belgian league

SC Charleroi won the Belgian league in Verviers last Saturday, finishing six points clear of second place Rochefort TS. Meanwhile, JSC Rochefort B won Division 2, although will not gain promotion as B teams are barred from top flight table football. Google translate

Sudden death final at Michigan Satellite

A tightly contested final in the Michigan FISTF Satellite saw Erik Haefke win his first tournament, defeating Peter Alegi 4-3 in sudden death extra time, topping a field of nine players.
