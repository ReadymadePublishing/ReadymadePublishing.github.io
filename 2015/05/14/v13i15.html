International Edition

Editorial

Early reports suggest that the Haas brothers have had rather a lot of success in the past fortnight, with Alexander winning the Austrian IO, and Christian taking the Spanish GP, whilst finding time to lift a WASPA open in between.

Headlines

Traiskirchen title for Haas

Alexander Haas won the open title at the IO Traiskirchen on 2nd May, beating Alexander Ruf in the final, with Christian Haas and Björn Kegenbein dropping out of the competition at the semi-final stages. TFC Mattersburg beat Berlin side Sparta Spreeathen to take the team title. Full results and report here [de] (Google translate)

Christian Haas wins Spanish GP

Details are a bit thin on the ground, but TFC Wiener Neustadt have reported that Christian Haas won the Spanish GP held in Mallorca last weekend, beating Belgian player Justin Leroy in the final.

Victory in Gillingham for Rémi Soret

AS Hennuyer’s Frenchman Rémi Soret beat clubmate David Vantassel 2-0 to lift the Gillingham IO title, whilst Greek side Proteas Athens won the team event after a narrow 1-0 victory over Yorkshire Phoenix.
