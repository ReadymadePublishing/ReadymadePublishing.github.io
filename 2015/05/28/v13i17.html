Editorial

There's not been an awful lot of tournament action this, with only the Austrian category championships taking place, but thankfully a number of interesting articles have appeared, ranging from a preview of the FISTF Major in Bologna, all the way through to a rather peculiar statement from the Brazilian association..

Headlines

Stefan Sandner becomes Austrian veteran champion

The first ‘Austrian Champions Day, which sees the national championships for the Veteran, U19, U15 and female categories played out, saw Stefan Sander walk to victory, comfortably dispatching Erich Hinklemann 4-1 in the veteran final. Meanwhile Lukas Eppensteiner (U19), Daniel Vranovitz (U12) and Ivan Gutl (Female) were also victorious. (Google translate)

World Cup Entries Open

Entries for the 2015 World Cup in Italy are now open, according to the official FISTF site.
