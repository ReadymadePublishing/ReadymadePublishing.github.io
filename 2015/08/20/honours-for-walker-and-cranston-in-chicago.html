Editorial

Eagles Napoli have completed the signing of Vasco Guimarães from SC Valletta this week. Along with fellow new signing Antonio Mettivieri, and home-grown star Massimo Bolognino, it further asserts the dominance of Italian clubs within the game.

Headlines

Honours go to Walker and Cranston in Chicago

Zach Walker & Daniel Cranston both left the windy city with silverware, with Walker taking home his seventh National Championship title, beating Paul Eyes 2-1 in the final. Walker beat Eyes again the following day in the semi-final of the International Open of Chicago, only to lose 2-1 to Daniel Cranston at the final hurdle. The was some consolation for the unfortunate Paul Eyes, however, as he became US Veteran National Champion with a 1-0 victory in the final, against Peter Alegi.

Murcilli wins International Open in Las Carolinas

Argentinian Claudio Murcilli topped a field of eight to take the first International Open to be staged in his home country after defeating Sebastian Pesci 2-1 in the final.
