Headlines

Christie lifts Scottish Cup

There was joy in Burntisland for Dundee United player Gareth Christie last weekend, as he beat Manchester TFC’s Malcolm Lees 2-0 in the final to lift the Scottish Cup, having defeated Andy Beskaby in the semi-finals. Lees himself had overcome strong opposition in the form of Dundee United’s Greg Dand and Glasgow’s Tom Burns, but fell short at the final hurdle.

The Argus: Volume 14

Volume 13 of The Argus will draw to a close at the end of this month, making way for the imaginatively titled ‘Volume 14’.

You may have noticed that there’s been a few interviews, and other articles, appearing over the past few weeks. The plan, along with a few visual tweaks to the design, is to include more articles in the newsletter:- expect features on the return of the Scottish national team to the World Cup, the Italian Club League, and an opinion piece on the future of the game in the UK to appear early on.

If you have any ideas for topics that could be covered, feel that you could provide an insight in to the game through an interview, have some news but nowhere to publish it, or would like to contribute a piece yourself, please get in touch by replying to this email. Anything Subbuteo-related will be considered!
