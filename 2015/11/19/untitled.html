Belgian GP title for Dejardin

Valéry Dejardin won the Belgian Grand Prix in Rochefort, defeating fellow countryman Sébastien Scheen in the final in a tournament which saw each of the semi-final berths taken by Belgian players. Pascal Scheen went one better, beating Frenchman Eric Lienhardt to lift the veteran category title, whilst ST Stembert lifted the team title.
