Editorial

It's unfortunate that, with the news of George Drazinakas' resignation from the FISTF Board following events at the Champions League, Gábor Baross' splendid achievement in Austria - becoming the first Hungarian player to reach a semi-final outside of their native country - falls down the news pecking order. Nevertheless, it's a marvellous achievement for one of the sport's newest nations.

Headlines

FISTF Sports Director Resigns

Reports on the UK Subbuteo Forum say that George Drazinakas has resigned as FISTF Sports Director following events at the FISTF Champions League held in Belgium last weekend. Mike Parnaby added that both Drazinakas and the tournament organiser and former Sports Director Olivier Pere suffered from a range of controversial incidents, including red cards being ignored, rule breaking, and banned players taking part.
