Editorial

There's been success for Válery Dejardin in an exciting Belgian championship, whilst Den Mulia scored an impressive victory to lift the International Open of Singapore. Elsewhere, there's been big wins for Mat Lampitt and Cédric Garnier.

Headlines

Valéry Dejardin crowned Subbuteo King of Belgium

Rochefort’s Válery Dejardin topped closely fought group of six to claim the 2014/15 Belgian Championship, with five of the six separated by just three points. David Fraikin topped the second division. Google Translate translation

Den Mulia takes 2015 TFAS Cup

Local player Den Mulia lifted the International Open of Singapore, defeating an impressive field including Stephane Lambert (Belgium), as well as players from Australia, Hong Kong, Indonesia, and Malaysia.
