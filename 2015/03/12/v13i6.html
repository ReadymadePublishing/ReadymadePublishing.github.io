Editorial

This weekend saw the world's biggest tournament, the Major of Frameries, bring over 200 players and 38 teams to compete in Mons, with hosts AS Hennuyer and local club SC Eugies putting out an incredible 9 teams between them!.

Headlines

Emanuele Licheri wins in Mons

Italian star Emanuele Licheri won arguably the biggest prize outside of the world cup last weekend in a spectacular 4-3 victory Angelo Borg from Malta, whilst his club - Reggio Emilia - also lifted the team trophy by the narrowest of margins, beating Borg’s club Valletta Lions by 8 goals to 6 after the two teams held eachother to a 2-2 stalemate.
